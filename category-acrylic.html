Asiee paintings

The juicy fruits

My favorite part of the painting is the lemon and banana, maybe it is because I like yellow, but I think the shadow and color really good on those parts. The hardest part to paint is the pineapple because it has a lot of detail and I am using a lot of colors just for it.



Cloudy neighborhood

I am inspired by @tiinasdairy paint on TikTok, I add gloomy cloud vibes. because the story that I want to show is about that day a girl in the yellow apartment building is planning to take a walk and hang out with friends that day. So she hang Teru teru bōzu, hoping for the rain cloud would go away.



Water Lily up high

For the first time, I am using one dominant colour in my painting, blue, which is not like using such a colourful palette. now I challenge myself just to use a minor variation of colour. this painting was made on 10th September 2022, thanks to Dan Scott and Amita Dand that give me resources also inspiration.



Teary waterfall

These days seem to be peaceful and calm, so I decide to paint something that resembles my feeling. Therefore I painted this beautiful waterfall, inspired by Correa Art. this painting was made on 17th September 2022, and as usual on my weekend I painted it. This is my very first landscape painting because before I usually did portraits on canvas.
