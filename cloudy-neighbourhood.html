cloudy neighborhood

this is my second painting on canvas, I painted it on 4th September 2022. It took me two weeks to finish the painting, not because it is hard but because I am inconsistent to paint it. I am inspired by @tiinasdairy paint on TikTok, I add gloomy cloud vibes. because the story that I want to show is about that day a girl in the yellow apartment building is planning to take a walk and hang out with friends that day. So she hang Teru teru bōzu, hoping for the rain cloud would go away.


my friends and family have quite unique reactions to my paintings. they thought that a white little thing hanging in the window is a ghost. I said no, that is teru-teru. Just for you guys to know my favorite part of the painting is the tiny teru-teru and the little garden on the corner. And the least favorite part is the cloud, why? because it was the hardest part to paint. I hope I am getting better at painting a cloud.
